The CEO actively promotes and models ethical behavior within the organization by setting a clear tone at the top, consistently demonstrating integrity in decision-making, and openly communicating the importance of ethical standards. They lead by example, adhering to the organization's values and expectations, which encourages employees to follow suit. Additionally, the CEO establishes and enforces comprehensive ethics policies, provides ongoing ethics training, and creates safe channels for employees to voice concerns or report unethical conduct. By holding themselves and others accountable, the CEO reinforces a culture of transparency, trust, and ethical responsibility, ultimately strengthening the organization's cultural foundation.
