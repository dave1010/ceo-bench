The CEO ensures that ethical considerations and corporate responsibility are integrated into strategic decision-making by establishing a strong organizational culture rooted in ethical principles. This includes setting clear values and expectations aligned with social responsibility, promoting transparency and open communication, and embedding ethical guidelines into policies and procedures. The CEO also champions stakeholder engagement by considering the needs and perspectives of employees, customers, communities, and investors. Additionally, they oversee training programs to reinforce ethical behavior and ensure compliance with laws and standards. By demonstrating commitment through their actions and decisions, the CEO fosters an environment where ethical considerations are central to strategic planning, risk management, and overall corporate objectives.
